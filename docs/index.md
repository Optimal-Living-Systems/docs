# Optimal Living Systems — Documentation

**OLS is a mutual aid nonprofit building open-source AI infrastructure for communities.** This site is the central knowledge base for everyone who works on, collaborates with, or wants to understand the OLS ecosystem — from new volunteers joining their first sprint to external organizations exploring our published frameworks.
